Get to know me through my work with Visual Design

I greatly enjoy visual design because I see it was a way for me to express myself creatively on a large scale. There is a lot of pressure creating something that will be used as the face of a company, but that is exactly why I love it.

Vance Services logo

This is a logo that I made for my company Vance Services. Vance services is a landscaping and Proporty management company. The logo is supposed to two blades of grass and a hand saw in the shape of a "V" for Vance.

Artaius logo

This is a logo that I made for a company start-up company named Artaius Outfitters. Artaius means bear god and the company product is an electronically heated vest. The logo can be interpreted as a bear claw, a mountain with fire on it, or an "A" on top of an "O"

Surf the East Coast logo

This is a logo that I made for a website called Surf the East Coast. The website is supposed to be a one-stop-shop for people who want to get into surfing on the east coast. The logo is a wave that has "E" "C" for east coast inside of it.

Madison OS logo

This is a logo that I made for a website called Madison OS. Madison OS is an office hour scheduling system for both instructors and students. The logo is a simple calender with an "M" for madison at the bottom.

Clean-Moor logo

This is a logo that I made for a company called Clean-Moor. Clean-Moor is a self cleaning buoy that boats tie off to instead of anchoring out. The logo is a "C" and an "M" for Clean-Moor in the shape of the product itself.

S4L logo

This is a logo that I made for a website called Sustain 4 Life. Sustain 4 Life is alternative way to shop sustainably via website and extension browser. The logo is simply and "S", "4", and "L" for sustain for life
